This is regards to a series of workshops I did to teach people basic reinforcement learning concepts. We go over fundamentals of RL, such as 
MDP's, Explore vs Exploit, Q and Value functions, and more. It was taught for the purposes of implementation, and was made to be quite approachable. 

This was taught in March->April of 2024 to the UVicAI Club.
